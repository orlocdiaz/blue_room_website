Frequently Asked Questions

What is the estimated delivery time?
The engineer working on your project will tell you the estimated delivery time. But we always try to work efficiently delivering projects as soon as possible while mantaining the quality.
What payment methods are available?
You can pay at the studio with any type of card or in cash. But we also offer you the option to make your reservation and pay online, so you can focus on what really matters whe you get to the studio. If you require to pay in any other way please contact us.
Do you work with remote projects?
Yes, we do work with remote projects, you can send us your recording to edit, mix or master it trough a platform that doesn't compress the files. For remote projects anticipated payment is required.
What are our rates?
Our rates vary from project to project and from the services you require. Also our rates include the engineer salary plus the use of space and gear. You can send and email or call the studio and we will give you a more precise estimation.
